Team Experience

Queensland Junior Track Team
Dec 2016 - Feb 2017

Here I trained with James Moriaty - Australian U17 Individual Pursuit Champion (2017) - and the QAS in preperation for the Junior Track National Titles in Sydney.

Queensland Junior Road Team
Jun 2017 - Oct 2017

Here I was coached by Louise Jones and Brad Reardon for the Junior Road National Titles in Tasmania. At the titles I competed in the Time Trial, Road Race and Criterium. In the Road Race I ran 4th in a wet and windy race, one also that was heavily affected by crashs. I was one of the few who were smart and skillful enough to make it through unscathed.

Balmoral Elite Team
Mar 2017 - Now

I've been a part of this team for a while now. I consider myself one of the better riders due to my ability to preform on any course. I have placed in both the White Ribbon Crit and in the Metropolitan Champs - the most hilly race in Queensland.
